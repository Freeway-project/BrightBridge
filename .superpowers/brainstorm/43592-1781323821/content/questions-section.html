A — Inline per issue

Each flagged item has an "Ask about this" button. The question is linked to that specific checklist item. The TA sees which item triggered the question.

  • Questions have context — no ambiguity
  • TA sees exactly what triggered it
  • Harder to ask a general question
  • More complex to build

B — Dedicated section

A separate "My Questions" accordion section shows all questions + replies, with a button to ask another. General or specific — instructor's choice.

  • One place for all Q&A history
  • Works for general and specific questions
  • Simpler to build (existing issue model)
  • No direct link to the flagged item

  • Conversation feels natural
